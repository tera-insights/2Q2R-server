Steps to authenticate:

  1. Check the phone for a push notification
  2. Alternatively, scan QR code with 2Q2R App
  3. Verify that the challenge is the same
  4. Press OKAY button on the phone
  5. Different challenge? Notify administator

Why check the challenge?
Chance of login highjacking reduced to 1 in 281 trillions for first 8 characters and 1 in 374 trillion trillion trillion trillions for all 28 characters.
