FIDO U2F Device

FIDO U2F keys use a FIDO U2F security key to authenticate. This method constitutes of inserting the key into a USB slot and pressing the button on the key.

The following U2F devices will work with 2Q2R:
  • Yubico FIDO U2F
  • HyperSecu
  • Happlink plug-up
